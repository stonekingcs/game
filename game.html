How to Play

Monster Survivors is an exciting survival game where you must defend yourself against endless waves of monsters. Use your weapons and special abilities to stay alive as long as possible. Collect power-ups and upgrade your character to become stronger and face increasingly challenging waves of enemies.

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Click to shoot at monsters
  • Collect power-ups to enhance your abilities
  • Survive as long as possible to achieve high scores
